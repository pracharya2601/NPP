# Code of conduct

We are committed to a welcoming, respectful, and harassment-free community.

Participants must:

- communicate constructively and assume good faith;
- respect differing experience levels, backgrounds, and viewpoints;
- critique ideas and code rather than people;
- avoid harassment, discrimination, threats, doxxing, and sexualized conduct;
- protect customer, merchant, and contributor privacy.

Maintainers may edit, remove, or reject contributions and temporarily or permanently restrict participation when behavior violates these expectations. Enforcement decisions should be proportional, documented privately, and avoid exposing reporters unnecessarily.

Report conduct concerns privately to the maintainers through the repository's private security/contact channel. If the concern involves a maintainer, contact another maintainer where possible.

This policy applies in project repositories, discussions, review threads, community chat, events, and public interactions made on behalf of the project.
